**Sehaat Saathi** is a comprehensive, AI-driven healthcare ecosystem designed to bring expert medical guidance to every Indian household. By combining cutting-edge Large Language Models (LLMs) with localized health data, it provides symptom analysis, medicine information, and emergency assistanceall within a visually stunning, easy-to-use interface.

---

##  Key Features

###  14+ Specialized AI Doctor Agents
Consult with specialized virtual experts including:
- **Dr. Sehaat (General Physician):** For daily health issues and OTC guidance.
- **Dr. Hriday (Cardiologist):** Heart health, BP management, and ECG analysis.
- **Vaidya Veda (Ayurveda):** Traditional Indian Wisdom meets modern AI.
- **Dr. Sthree (Gynecologist):** Compassionate women's health and maternal care.
- *Also including: Neurologist, Pediatrician, Dermatologist, ENT, Psychiatrist, and even a Clinical Pharmacist!*

###  Jan Aushadhi Savings Calculator (India's 1st)
Stop overpaying for medicines! Compare expensive branded drug prices with **Government of India's PMBJP (Jan Aushadhi)** generic alternatives. Save up to 70-90% on your medical bills and find the nearest Jan Aushadhi Kendra instantly.

###  Dadi Ma AI: Smart Home Remedies
Get trusted \"Ghar ke Nuskhe\" for common ailments like cough, cold, or acidity. Every remedy is cross-verified by AI to ensure it is safe and effective, with multi-lingual audio support.

###  Medical Report & Medicine Scan (OCR)
- **Report Analysis:** Upload blood tests or scans (PDF/Image) to get a simplified, jargon-free explanation.
- **Medicine Label Scan:** Uses **Tesseract OCR** to identify medicine names from photos and provide dosage/safety alerts.

###  Real-Time Emergency & OPD Network
- **Hospital Finder:** Real-time data of hospitals across Bihar, Jharkhand, UP, and major Metros.
- **Digital OPD Ticket:** Generate a PDF OPD slip with a token number to reduce wait times at hospitals.

###  AI Workout & Diet Planner
- Get personalized **Weight Loss, Muscle Building, or Yoga** routines.
- AI-generated Indian diet plans based on your BMI, age, and health conditions.

##  How It Works

1. **Patient Profiling:** The system starts by taking your age, weight, and history to provide context-aware medical advice.
2. **Specialized Inference:** Your query is routed to the specific AI Doctor Agent using advanced Prompt Engineering.
3. **Safety First:** The AI is programmed with strict \"Red Flag\" protocols, recommending emergency care for life-threatening symptoms.
4. **Actionable Outputs:** Get PDF prescriptions, .ics reminders for meds, and map links for hospitals.
